## Data Authority and Limitations

### Source Authority

This Tool aggregates data from official Polish legal databases. However:

| Source Type | Authority Level | Use Case |
|------------|----------------|----------|
| **Official government databases (ISAP, Dziennik Ustaw)** | Authoritative | Statute text (primary reference) |
| **Community/open-data sources** | Supplementary | Case law, cross-references (verify independently) |
| **EUR-Lex / treaty databases** | Authoritative for EU/international law | EU law and international obligations metadata |

**CRITICAL**: Community-maintained resources are **NOT** official government publications. While generally reliable, they may contain transcription errors, outdated information, or incomplete coverage.

### Commercial Legal Databases

For professional legal work, **always cross-check with commercial legal databases** such as:

- LEX (Wolters Kluwer), Legalis (C.H. Beck), LexisNexis Polska

These services provide editorial oversight, comprehensive annotations, and professional-grade currency guarantees that this Tool **cannot** provide.

## Currency and Staleness

### No Real-Time Updates

- Database updates are **manual** and may lag official publications by **weeks or months**
- Polish law changes continuously through new statutes (ustawy), amendments (nowelizacje), and court decisions (orzeczenia)
- **Last-updated timestamps** in tool responses indicate data age, but should be treated as unreliable

### Staleness Warnings

The Tool includes staleness warnings when data is >30 days old. **These warnings are not exhaustive**:

- Even "fresh" data may be outdated if recent amendments were published in the Dziennik Ustaw
- Staleness warnings do not guarantee completeness or accuracy
- Always check official sources for the current legal position

### Amendments and Repeals

- A provision marked "in force" (obowiązujący) may have been:
  - Amended (zmieniony) after the last database update
  - Repealed (uchylony) but not yet removed from the database
  - Subject to transitional provisions (przepisy przejściowe) affecting its applicability
  - Declared unconstitutional by the Trybunał Konstytucyjny (Constitutional Tribunal)
- The Tool does **NOT** automatically track amendments in real-time

## Coverage Gaps

### What's Missing

This Tool does **NOT** include:

1. **Complete case law (orzecznictwo)** -- Coverage of Supreme Court (Sąd Najwyższy), Supreme Administrative Court (NSA), and lower court decisions is limited
2. **Historical statute versions** -- Limited availability of historical provision wording (wersje historyczne)
3. **Legal commentary (komentarze)** -- No editorial annotations, academic commentary, or practice guides
4. **Unpublished decisions** -- Lower court decisions and administrative rulings largely absent
5. **Full legislative history** -- Limited coverage of uzasadnienia projektów ustaw and parliamentary debates (stenogramy sejmowe)
6. **EU law implementation details** -- Transposition tables and EU directive implementation notes may be incomplete
7. **Local government acts (akty prawa miejscowego)** -- Municipal and voivodeship regulations are not covered

### Impact on Professional Use

These gaps mean:

- **Incomplete Legal Research**: Tool results are inherently incomplete and may miss critical authorities
- **Context Missing**: Without komentarze and uzasadnienia, interpretation may be incorrect
- **EU Law Integration**: Poland's EU membership means many provisions implement EU directives; without full transposition context, analysis may be incomplete
- **Constitutional Tribunal Gaps**: Without complete Trybunał Konstytucyjny rulings, provisions that have been declared unconstitutional may appear as valid
